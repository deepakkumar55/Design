Settings

  • Click on a pear to select it
  • Click on another pear to connect them with a line
  • Lines can only be drawn horizontally or vertically
  • Lines cannot cross through other pears
  • Connect all pears to complete the level

Keyboard Shortcuts:

Enter or Space: Connect selected pears

Esc: Cancel selection

R: Reset level
